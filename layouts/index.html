Features

  • Fully configurable

    Set your own focus duration, short break, long break, and rounds per cycle. Global shortcuts let you start, pause, and skip rounds without switching windows.

  • Session statistics

    Every completed session is logged. Review your day at a glance, track weekly streaks, and explore your full history with a 52-week heatmap.

  • 37 themes — or your own

    Ships with Nord, Dracula, Catppuccin, Gruvbox, Tokyo Night, and more. Drop a JSON file into the themes folder for a custom theme. Changes apply instantly, no restart required.

  • Runs quietly in the background

    Minimize or close to tray and keep the timer running. A dynamic tray icon shows your progress arc in real time, and desktop notifications handle round transitions.

  • Seven languages

    Auto-detects your OS language. Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, and Portuguese.
